Cookies

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them every time you visit the site.

What types of cookies do we use?

We use both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which stay on your device until they expire or you delete them. We also use first-party cookies, which are set by our site, and third-party cookies, which are set by other websites and services that we use.

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Analyzing site traffic and usage to improve our site's performance and user experience
  • Personalizing content and ads to better meet your interests and needs
  • Remembering your preferences and settings so you don't have to keep re-entering themce
  • Providing social media features and to analyze our traffic.

Your options

You can choose to accept or decline cookies when you first visit our site or by adjusting your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our site.
