Online reputation refers to how your brand is perceived and talked about online including reviews, ratings, and comments on social media or other online platforms. Learn more about ORM here.

Online presence management refers to the process of controlling and maintaining your business's digital footprint across various online channels, including social media, review sites, search engines, and more. It involves actively managing your brand's online reputation, building a strong online presence, and engaging with your target audience. By leveraging the power of online presence management, you can increase brand awareness, generate more leads and sales, and establish your business as a credible and authoritative player in your industry.
